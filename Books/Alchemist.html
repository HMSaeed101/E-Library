Description

The Alchemist is a timeless novel about an Andalusian shepherd named Santiago who has a recurring dream about treasure hidden near the pyramids in Egypt. Encouraged by an old king and guided by signs and omens, he sets off on a journey across deserts, through danger and self-discovery, to find his Personal Legend.

Blending adventure, mysticism, and life lessons, Paulo Coelho's masterpiece explores themes of destiny, courage, listening to one's heart, and recognizing the universal language of the world. With millions of copies sold worldwide, it continues to inspire readers to pursue their dreams and recognize the opportunities life presents.
