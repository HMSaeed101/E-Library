Description

The Sealed Nectar (Ar-Raheeq Al-Makhtum) is widely regarded as one of the most authentic, comprehensive, and beautifully written biographies of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ in modern times. Authored by the renowned Indian scholar Safiur Rahman Mubarakpuri, this book won first prize in the Muslim World League-sponsored international competition for the best biography of the Prophet ﷺ held in 1979.

The book covers key events such as the Year of the Elephant, his childhood, prophethood, the Meccan period of struggle, the Hijrah, the establishment of the Islamic state in Madinah, major battles, treaties, and his final sermon.

Written with clarity, reverence, and scholarly precision, The Sealed Nectar avoids exaggeration while highlighting the Prophet’s exemplary character, mercy, wisdom, leadership, and universal message. It has become a standard reference for students of Seerah, Islamic history enthusiasts, and anyone seeking to understand the life and legacy of the final Messenger of Allah ﷺ. This edition offers a smooth English translation suitable for both new readers and those deepening their knowledge.
