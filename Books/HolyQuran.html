Description

The Holy Quran is the sacred scripture of Islam - the literal word of Allah revealed to Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) over 23 years. Containing 114 Surahs and over 6,000 verses, it provides complete guidance on faith, worship, ethics, law, family, society, and the purpose of life. Renowned for its unmatched linguistic beauty, timeless wisdom, and message of mercy, justice, and monotheism, the Quran remains unchanged and is recited, memorized, and studied by millions worldwide. This edition offers the original Arabic text in a clean, readable format for daily reflection and learning.
